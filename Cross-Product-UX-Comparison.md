# Cross Product UX Comparison

## Products Considered
- Amazon
- Google Pay
- LinkedIn

## Common UX Patterns Observed
- Clean onboarding process
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Focus on user engagement

## Key Differences
- Amazon focuses on product discovery and purchase flow
- Google Pay focuses on speed and simplicity
- LinkedIn focuses on professional networking and content

## UX Challenges Noticed
- Content overload in LinkedIn
- Navigation complexity for new users in Amazon
- Security concerns awareness in payment apps

## Learning Outcome
Comparing multiple products helped me understand how
different business goals shape user experience decisions.
